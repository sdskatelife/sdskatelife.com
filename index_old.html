About us...
SD Skate Life was established by Andrew Barbosa in 2013 after spending seven years directing summer skateboarding camps and teaching private lessons. The goal of our site is to connect beginning skateboarders to passionate instructors with extensive recreation experience in San Diego County.
Our mission is to create a network of skateboarding education services in order to inspire the next gerneration of skateboarders in safe and healthy environments.
Why SD Skate Life?
We are a skateboarder owned and operated business that is dedicated to helping you or your children learn how to skate in a safe, fun, and healthy environment. We know that skateboarding can be an intimidating sport, and that skate parks can be equally intimidating. All of our instructors are passionate and experienced teachers who will stop at nothing to help you build control and confidence on a skateboard.
Philosophy
Our philosophy is that building confidence is the most crucial element of learning how to skateboard. All of our programs are centered around helping students develop proper technique, skate park etiquette, and a fundamental understanding of skateboarding safety so that our students are able feel confident and comfortable when they enter a skate park for the first time on their own.
Our Programs
Whether you are interested in private lessons, group lessons, or classes, all of our programs are designed to introduce you to all the fundamentals of skateboarding in four quick sessions. Students will learn anywhere from 2-10 tricks per hour and will graduate the program with confidence and a sense of accomplishment.
Instructors
SD Skate Life will connect you with a experienced and knowledgeable instructor in your area. Our certified instructors are passionate skateboarders who have years of experience teaching skateboarding!
Head Instructor Andrew Barbosa has been skateboarding for well over a decade. He has carried professional sponsors and has competed in nationally televised contests throughout his career. He has been instructing skateboarding for seven years now through private lessons, camps, and clinics. Andrew started SD Skate Life to expand skateboarding education and make it accessible to anyone in San Diego.
