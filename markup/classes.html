Skate Classes
SD Skatelife is now offering Skateboarding Classes at many locations in San Diego. We proudly offer our class programs in partnership with the City of Oceanside, City of Poway, City of Chula Vista, and City of San Diego Parks and Recreation Departments.
Our classes are open to participants ages 5-14 and are guaranteed to show significant improvements in skill, coordination, and confidence. We offer both beginner and intermediate/advanced programs to meet the varying skill levels of our students. Click the links below to get involved today!
