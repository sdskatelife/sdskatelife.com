Encinitas Skateboarding Classes
Encinitas Skate Park 425 Santa Fe Dr
Come learn to skate and develop your tricks at the Encinitas Skate Park! This course focuses on safety techniques, fundamentals, skate park etiquette, and trick development. Classes are offered for kids ages 5-11 years old and any skill level. Our classes are guaranteed to help show improvement in balance, confidence, and coordination. Students will learn how to skate in a safe, healthy, and productive environment!
