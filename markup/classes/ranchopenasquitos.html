Rancho Penasquitos Skateboarding Classes
Rancho Penasquitos Skate Park 10111 Carmel Mountain Rd.
Come learn to skate and develop your tricks at the Rancho Penasquitos Skate Park! This course focuses on safety techniques, fundamentals, skate park etiquette, and trick development. Classes are offered for kids ages 5-14 years old and any skill level. Our classes are guaranteed to help show improvement in balance, confidence, and coordination. Students will learn how to skate in a safe, healthy, and productive environment that will be closed to the public while the class runs!
