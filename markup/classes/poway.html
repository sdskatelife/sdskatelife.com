Poway Skateboarding Classes

Poway Skateboard Park 13090 Civic Center Dr.
SUMMER SESSION
Each Wednesday from 5:00-6:00PM
Intro to Skateboarding: July 16th - September 3rd
Come learn to skate and develop your tricks at the Poway Skate Park this Summer! This course focuses on safety techniques, fundamentals, skate park etiquette, and trick development. Classes are offered for kids ages 6-13 years old and any skill level. Our classes are guaranteed to help show improvement in balance, confidence, and coordination. Students will learn how to skate in a safe, healthy, and productive environment that will be closed to the public while the class runs!
Cost: $95 Residents/ $105 NR
*Skateboard rentals are available upon request!
Sign up in person at the Poway Community Center located at 13094 Civic Center Dr. Poway, CA 92064
Sign up online at www.poway.org/classes
