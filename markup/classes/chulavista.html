Chula Vista Skateboarding Classes
Montevalle Skateboard Park •  Salt Creek Skateboard Park
We offer classes at both of Chula Vista's beautiful skate parks! Come learn to skate and develop your tricks. This course focuses on safety techniques, fundamentals, skate park etiquette, and trick development. Classes are offered for kids ages 6-13 years old and any skill level. Our classes are guaranteed to help show improvement in balance, confidence, and coordination. Students will learn how to skate in a safe, healthy, and productive environment that will be closed to the public while the class runs!
Sign Up!
Visit Chula Vista Recreation for schedule, price and to sign up!
