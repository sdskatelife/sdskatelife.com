Oceanside Skateboarding Classes
Alex Road Skate Park
Come learn to skate and develop your tricks at the Alex Road Skate Park in Oceanside! This course focuses on safety techniques, fundamentals, skate park etiquette, and trick development. Classes are offered for kids ages 6-16 years old and any skill level. Our classes are guaranteed to help show improvement in balance, coordination, and skill in just four sessions!
