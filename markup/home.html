Welcome!
We are a skateboarder owned and operated company dedicated to helping you or your children learn how to skate in a safe, fun, and healthy environment. Our goal is to connect beginning skateboarders with passionate instructors who have extensive experience teaching new skateboarders how to build balance, coordination and confidence both on and off a skateboard.
We strive to offer the absolute best skateboarding education services in San Diego County! Our affordable camps, classes, and private lessons are designed to help make you feel comfortable and at ease. Whether you are a total beginner or are looking to fine-tune advanced skills, we have the right programs for you!
