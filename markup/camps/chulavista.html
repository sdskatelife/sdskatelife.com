Chula Vista Skateboarding Camps
Montevalle Skateboard Park •  Salt Creek Skateboard Park
Join the fun this summer at our Chula Vista Skateboard Camps. We offer camps at both of Chula Vista's beautiful skate parks! Join for a week or join them all and get a unique experience at each skate park! This camp focuses on teaching the fundamentals of skateboarding through exercises that will build balance, confidence and coordination. Come play skateboarding games, make new friends, meet special guest Pros and learn new tricks!
Sign Up!
Visit Chula Vista Recreation for schedule, price and to sign up!
Sign up in person at the Montevalle Community Center located at 840 Duncan Ranch Rd, Chula Vista, CA 91914 or the Salt Creek Community Center located at 2710 Otay Lakes Rd, Chula Vista, CA 91915
*Skateboard rentals are available upon request!
