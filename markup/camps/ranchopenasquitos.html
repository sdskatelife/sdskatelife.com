Join in on the fun this Summer at our Rancho Penasquitos Skate Camp! Come play skateboarding games, make new friends, and learn new tricks at the freshly renovated Rancho PQ Skate Park. Students will learn the fundamentals of skateboarding through exercises designed to build balance, confidence, and coordination. The park will be CLOSED to any other users during the camp to ensure a safe, productive, and fun environment for your kids to learn how to skateboard!
Cost: $125 per week
