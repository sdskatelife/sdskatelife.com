Join in on the fun this Summer at our Oceanside Skateboard Camps. We are offering camps at all three of Oceanside's beautiful skate parks! Join for a week, or join for all four and get a unique experience at each skate park! This camp focuses on teaching the fundamentals of skateboarding through exercises that will build balance, confidence, and coordination. Come play skateboarding games, make new friends, meet special guest pro's, and learn new tricks!
Cost: $150 Residents / $160 NR
*Skateboard rentals are available upon request!
