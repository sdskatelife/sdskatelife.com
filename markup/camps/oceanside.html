Oceanside Skateboarding Camp
Alex Road Skate Park •  Melba Bishop Skate Park  •  MLK Skate Park
Join the fun this summer at our Oceanside Skateboard Camps. We offer camps at all three of Oceanside's beautiful skate parks! Join for a week or join them all and get a unique experience at each skate park! This camp focuses on teaching the fundamentals of skateboarding through exercises that will build balance, confidence and coordination. Come play skateboarding games, make new friends, meet special guest Pros and learn new tricks!
